About me.

My name's Felipe Guimarães. I'm from Brazil. I started on Roblox as a player in 2015, and by 2017 I had Studio open, teaching myself how games are built.

Since then I've worked across movement, backend, frontend, AI, and UI. I've shipped games solo and led the scripting on titles played by tens of thousands of players at once, with a focus on systems that hold up at scale.

  1. 2015First time on Roblox, as a player.
